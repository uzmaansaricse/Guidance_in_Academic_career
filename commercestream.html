Graduation options in Commerce

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

A classic choice that allows students to specialize in areas like accounting, finance, and taxationSpecializations: B.Com General, B.Com in Accounting and Finance, B.Com in Corporate Secretaryship, etc.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A three to four year undergraduate degree program that covers subjects like accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, and operations management Specializations: Marketing, Finance, HR, International Business, etc.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Economics (BE)

A three-year degree course that allows students to study basic and advanced economics Specializations: Applied Economics, Business Economics, etc.
Duration: 3 years.

Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS)

A three-year undergraduate degree that allows students to enhance their management skills
Duration: 3 years.

Bachelor of Accounting and Finance (BAF)

A degree that combines the study of accounting and finance.
Duration: 3 years.

Bachelor of Financial Markets (BFM)

A course that allows students to learn about financial markets and related fields
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Economics (B.Econ)

Focuses on economic theory, micro and macroeconomics, econometrics, and other related subjects. Specializations: Applied Economics, Business Economics, etc.
Duration: 3 years

Chartered Accountancy (CA)

This is a professional course that qualifies you to become a chartered accountant. It involves a combination of theoretical study and practical training. Stages: Foundation, Intermediate, and Final exam, along with practical training.
Duration: 3 to 5 years (depending on the path and stages)

Company Secretary (CS)

A professional course that prepares you to handle the legal and regulatory affairs of a company, including governance, corporate laws, and secretarial practices.
Duration: 3 to 4 years

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Finance/Marketing/HR

A specialized version of the BBA that focuses on specific areas like finance, marketing, or human resources.
Duration: 3 years

Hotel Management

If you're interested in hospitality, this course teaches you about hotel operations, event management, and customer service.
Duration: 3 to 4 years

Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM)

Focuses specifically on the hospitality industry and prepares you for a career in hotels, resorts, and tourism.
Duration: 3 years

Integrated Law (BA LLB or B.Com LLB)

An integrated law degree that combines undergraduate courses in arts or commerce with a law degree. It opens career options in law and legal fields.
Duration: 5 years

Fashion Designing

For those interested in the fashion industry, this course focuses on design principles, textile studies, and fashion history.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Banking and Insurance (BBI)

Prepares you for a career in banking, insurance, and financial sectors by teaching subjects like risk management, insurance products, and financial analysis.
Duration: 3 years

Animation/Graphic Designing

For those interested in the creative industry, this course focuses on graphic design, animation, visual effects, and related skills.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Event Management

Teaches the principles of organizing events, such as corporate events, weddings, or entertainment events.
Duration: 3 years
