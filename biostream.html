Graduation options in Biology

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)

A graduation degree that leads to becoming a certified doctor.
Duration: 5.5 years.

BSc in Biotechnology

Focuses on using biological systems, organisms, or derivatives to make or modify products for specific uses. This course leads to careers in the biotech and pharmaceutical industries.
Duration: 3 years.

Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS)

This course is for those interested in becoming a dentist. It covers oral health, dental surgeries, and dental care management.
Duration: 5 years.

Bachelor of Science in Biology (BSc Biology)

A degree that provides a foundation in biological concepts like cellular biology, molecular
Duration: 3 years.

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing)

If you're interested in healthcare but want to focus on nursing, this degree will train you to become a registered nurse.
Duration: 4 years

BSc in Genetics

A course that covers topics like ecology, biodiversity, botany, and genetics.
Duration: 3 years.

BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery)

This program focuses on Ayurveda, a traditional Indian medical system. After completing this course, you can practice as an Ayurvedic doctor.
Duration: 5.5 years

BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery)

A course that focuses on homeopathy as an alternative medicine system. It covers the theory and practice of homeopathic treatments.
Duration: 5.5 years

BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)

This course prepares you for a career as a physiotherapist, focusing on physical rehabilitation, mobility, and pain management.
Duration: 4 years.

BAMS (Bachelor of Veterinary Science)

A degree for students passionate about animal health and care. It allows you to become a veterinarian.
Duration: 5 years

BSc in Biochemistry

A program that combines biology and chemistry, focusing on the chemical processes and substances that occur within living organisms.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Microbiology

This course focuses on microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and their impact on the environment, health, and industry. Graduates can work in research, pharmaceuticals, or healthcare.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Zoology / Botany

These courses focus on the study of animals (Zoology) or plants (Botany), preparing students for careers in research, conservation, and teaching.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Environmental Science

This course combines biology, chemistry, and environmental science to study environmental issues. Graduates can work in government, NGOs, or private industries in areas like sustainability and environmental protection.
Duration: 3 years

BPharma (Bachelor of Pharmacy)

For those interested in pharmaceuticals, this course focuses on drug development, production, and the study of medications.
Duration: 4 years.

BMLT (Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Technology)

This course is designed for students who want to work in medical diagnostics, focusing on laboratory tests, blood work, and clinical analysis.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Nutrition and Dietetics

This program is for those interested in food science, health, and wellness. It prepares you to become a nutritionist or dietitian, advising individuals or institutions on healthy eating habits.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Forensic Science

For students interested in criminal investigations, this course covers the scientific methods used in solving crimes, including DNA analysis, toxicology, and pathology.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Agricultural Science

This course focuses on the science of farming, crop production, and animal husbandry. It is ideal if you want to work in agriculture or agribusiness.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Genetic Engineering

A more advanced course, this degree focuses on the manipulation of organisms' genes for agricultural, medical, and industrial purposes.
Duration: 3 years.

BSc in Food Technology

This course combines biology and engineering to study food production, preservation, and quality control. It prepares you for a career in the food industry.
Duration: 3 years.
