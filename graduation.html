Graduation options in Arts

Bachelor of Arts (B.A.)

A three-year program that offers a wide range of subjects, such as psychology, political science, economics, English, history, geography, philosophy, social psychology, and sociology.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A 3-year program that covers business principles, organizational behavior, marketing, finance, human resource management, operations management, and entrepreneurship.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Fine Arts (B.F.A.)C

An undergraduate degree that covers fine arts, visual arts, and performing arts. Popular specializations include applied arts, painting and drawing, sculpture, film making, and ceramics.
Duration: 3 years

Integrated Law course (B.A + L.L.B.)

A combined course that offers both a Bachelor of Arts and a Bachelor of Laws.
Duration: 5 years (integrated course).

Bachelor of Journalism and Mass Communication (B.J.M.)

A course that can lead to a career in journalism.
Duration: 3 years

Bachelor of Hotel Management (B.H.M.)

A course that can lead to a career in hotel management.
Duration: 3/4 years

BA in Fashion Designing

This program is perfect if you're passionate about fashion and want to learn about designing, styling, fashion management, and textile arts.
Duration: 3/4 years.

B.Des (Bachelor of Design)

If you have an interest in design, whether in fashion, interior design, graphic design, or product design, this program will help you develop your creative skills.
Duration: 4 years.

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

If you're interested in technology but have an Arts background, BCA allows you to learn about computer programming, networking, and applications.
Duration: 3 years.

BA in Animation, Multimedia & Graphic Design

If you're interested in digital arts, animation, and graphic design, this course offers a solid foundation in creativity, design principles, and technology.
Duration: 3 years.

BA in Event Management

If you are organized and interested in planning events, this program covers event planning, marketing, communication, and management skills.
Duration: 3 years.

BSW (Bachelor of Social Work)

For those interested in social services, welfare work, and understanding the social issues that affect communities, BSW is an ideal course.
Duration: 3 years.

BA in Travel and Tourism Management

This program focuses on the travel and tourism industry, preparing you for careers in travel agencies, tour operators, and hospitality services.
Duration: 3 years.
