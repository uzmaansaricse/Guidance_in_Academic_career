Steps

1-Registration: Students register for counseling and pay any required fees.

2-Choice filling: Students select their preferred colleges and courses based on their ranking and available seats.

3-Seat allotment: Students are allotted seats in the colleges and courses they selected.

4-Document verification: Students verify their documents.

5-Fee payment: Students pay the admission fee.

6-Reporting: Students report to the college they were allotted to and complete the admission formalities.

Impotant Document needed for form filling

1-Valid ID Proof.

a- Aadhar Card/printout of e-Aadhar.
b- Voter's ID Card.
c- Driving License.
d- Passport.
e- PAN Card.

2-Medical.

3-Bank account passbook.

4-Passport size photos.

5-Class X Marksheet.

6-Class XII Marksheet.

7-Scan left & right thumb impression.

9-Signature.
