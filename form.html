Impotant points remeber during going for exams

1-Candidates are required to carry their photo identity proof and date of birth in original for verification at the examination centre.

2-The candidates will be required to reach their exam centre on their reporting time.

3-admit card is a mandatory document to be carried to the test centre.

4-All candidates should wear light-coloured sober clothes.

5-Candidates should wear open footwear like sandals and slippers instead of shoes.

6-Candidates should not wear clothes with full sleeves, big buttons etc.

7-Candidates should not wear heavy/traditional clothes.

8-No candidate should wear expensive/flashy accessories such as watches, bracelets, chains, earrings etc.

9-Female candidates should not leave their hair open during the exam.

10-Candidates are advised not to bring the prohibited items to the test centre.(Electroc items).
